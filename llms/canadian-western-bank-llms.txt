# Canadian Western Bank (CWB)

> Canadian Western Bank (CWB) is a Schedule I domestic chartered bank founded in 1988, headquartered in Edmonton, Alberta. In June 2024 National Bank of Canada agreed to acquire CWB (~CA$5B all-share deal); the acquisition closed in early 2025 and CWB now operates as a subsidiary of National Bank of Canada, with its brand being wound down. CWB publishes no first-party public developer portal and no documented public API. The entire cwbank.com domain now 301-redirects to National Bank of Canada (nbc.ca). This is an honest identity-only, aggregator-only record.

## Status
- No first-party public developer portal or documented public API.
- Developer/API hostnames (developer.cwbank.com, api.cwbank.com) do not resolve.
- cwbank.com 301-redirects to https://nbc.ca/en/business.html following the acquisition.
- No first-party FDX-conformant data-access API was found.
- Canada's Consumer-Driven Banking framework was legislated in 2024 (Budget 2024 / FCAC) but is not yet operational; no mandated open-banking API contract is in force.

## Data access
- Consumer-permissioned account/transaction data is reached only indirectly via third-party aggregators.
- Plaid is confirmed via external aggregator coverage directories.
- Flinks (National Bank-owned) is ecosystem context, not a documented first-party CWB integration.
